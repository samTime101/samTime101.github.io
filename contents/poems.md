### He who

always found me wandering ,
saw me as greedy as I can
and immoral as I can get

He saw me reading books,
praying him for my greedy self
,saw me myself calling poor
saw my greed and ignorant

Divide and rule , he liked
Diverse as he can get ,he made
Spawned some poor rats
Among some bloody snakes

He expected that rats will live
Though, he taught the snakes to kill

Whereas those snakes praying to kill

One at a time , slowly all went dying
Snake ate the last the rat
And the last snake went dying

Empty as it can get

That HE was me and
All i prayed was the evil me who was never free

HE never existed , and never will
the moment the last snake died
HE died

U may be that sheep
Among those bloody pigs
